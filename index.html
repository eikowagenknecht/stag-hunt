Always Stag: Always hunts stag.
Always Hare: Always hunts hare.
Tit-for-Tat: Starts by hunting stag, then mirrors the other player's previous choice.
Random: Randomly chooses stag or hare with 50% probability each round.
Cautious: Hunts hare when food is below the daily consumption threshold, otherwise hunts stag.
